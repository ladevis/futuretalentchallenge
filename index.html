Future Talent Challenge

The FUTURE TALENT CHALLENGE is a series of events and activities hosted by the Warehouse of Innovation. Bringing [tech] employers and employees, teachers and students, city and citizens together. To share existing assumptions and insights on their talent challenges, creatively challenge future perspectives and inspire a pragmatic plan of action that aims to positively impact the future talent landscape of the Netherlands.

Find Out More

The Warehouse of Innovation

The WAREHOUSE OF INNOVATION, located in the former V&D building in city center of Eindhoven, is a temporary space dedicated to showcasing and experiencing the latest developments in technology and innovation. Open to the public the Warehouse of Innovation sparks conversation on the future of technology, it’s impact on our daily lives, how technological developments take place and how we can play a role in this.

An invitation to join the discussion

The Future Talent Challenge extends an open invitation to anyone interested in sharing ideas and expertise on the future [tech] talent challenge. Whether currently experiencing or anticipating a future talent crunch; actively engaged with talent attraction and retention; or interested in future [tech] talent opportunities. You are invited to join in sharing insights and creatively challenging future perspectives; to help develop a pragmatic plan of action with the aim of positively impacting the future talent horizon of the Netherlands.

A kick-off Meet-up will provide an open platform to bring people together around the topic of future talent, whilst contributing to refining the format and content of the main event. With keynotes, workshops and networking the main Future Talent Challenge event will map the cross-fertilisation of different perspectives, with interaction and discussion designed to lead to new insights into the challenges of talent supply and demand. A working session will conclude by synthesizing the most relevant proposals to bridge the Future Talent Gap into a Vision, roadmap and Future Talent Plan of action.

02 FUTURE TALENT MAP

2-3 NOVEMBER
FRIDAY AND SATURDAY

The main EVENT – with keynotes and workshops will develop insights and map the key drivers behind a successful future talent strategy. Addressing three core themes impacting talent supply and demand:

LIVING This theme addresses the challenge of talent attraction and retention in an increasingly global market. How does successful city branding and quality of life impact talent supply? What other factors of living have an impact and what role should city, region or country play?

LEARNING This theme addresses the impact of education on future talent development. From primary to secondary school to life-long learning; from classical didactic to experiential or problem based. Or from apprenticeship to start-up accelerator. What are the most relevant approaches to bridge future talent supply and demand?

WORKING This theme looks into the demand side of talent from the perspective of industry, enterprise and entrepreneurs. Mapping [tech] trends and challenging assumptions about future jobs, capabilities and requirements. How well do we understand future talent demands?

Warehouse of Innovation, Vrijstraat 11, Eindhoven
