Future Talent Challenge

The future talent challenge is a series of events and activities bringing [tech] employers and employees, teachers and students, city and citizens together. To share existing assumptions and insights on their talent challenges, creatively challenging future perspectives and inspiring a plan of action that aims to have a positive impact on the future talent horizon of the Netherlands.

An invitation to join the discussion

The Future Talent Challenge extends an open invitation to anyone interested in contributing ideas, expertise and experience to a program that aims to uncover innovative and pragmatic approaches to address the future [tech] talent challenge.
